Some background!

posted on Monday, February 08, 2016 by @flashblu

I thought that I would give some context as to what this upcoming *thing* is! On Fri Jan 29, during the App.net #hackweekend, I pre-launched MyPageApp. It is powered by App.net, the social network and dev platform that puts users and developers first. I've been on App.net since May 2013, and I've had the pleasure of using several apps, interacting with other users, and seeing third-party devs happily supported by App.net founder @dalton and his vision for his product.

The main communication model for App.net is microblogging, where users post 256-character updates and snippets for their followers to see, and can likewise see posts from people that they themselves follow. I believe that this is a useful mode of communication and an important part of what has allowed a tight-knit community to flourish on App.net, but the proliferation of App.net microblogging has also made me think about what else could be built on App.net that is currently missing.

I don't know exactly what MyPageApp will look like, or all the things that it will do, but it will be about the user as a person. I hope that you will join me on this ride! You can subscribe to the mailing list below to receive updates as I continue to build it. Mention or private-message me @flashblu on App.net about anything, anytime!

- Flash
